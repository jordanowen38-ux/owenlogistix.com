About Owen Logistix LLC

Owen Logistix LLC is built on dedication, safety, and professionalism. The company has a clear growth plan and strong operational foundation, positioning Owen Logistix LLC to become a trusted regional logistics provider and build a profitable, sustainable operation in the Midwest freight market.

Based in Urbandale, Iowa, Owen Logistix LLC specializes in general freight delivery and hotshot loads across local, regional, and over-the-road (OTR) routes. We focus on reliable, on-time deliveries, professional logistics management, and long-term client partnerships.

Mission Statement

To deliver freight safely, efficiently, and professionally — providing consistent logistics solutions that help businesses move forward.

Vision Statement

To become a recognized Midwest carrier known for reliability, professionalism, and strong client relationships.

Our Services

General Freight Delivery

Reliable and secure transportation for a wide range of goods and materials across the Midwest.

Hotshot Loads

Expedited shipping for time-sensitive freight, tailored to meet urgent customer demands.

Load Board Sourcing

Strategic sourcing through DAT, TruckStop, and other dispatch networks to keep freight moving.
